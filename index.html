About

"You'll help me with this little problem, even if it involves some dirty work, won't you?

Responding to the call of an old friend, Natasha willingly headed to a place no mortal dare set foot on.

Murders, debt collection, selling information, those were classified as both business and hobby to the residents of the crime-ridden isle.

"A ruby has been stolen, and I want it back."

A one line note between the lines of her well used notepad.

The young reporter had 3 days to find the stolen gem before the consequence of the theft would send her client to a place worse than Hell.

However, one wrong move while collecting clues and gathering intel, and Natasha will find herself in the same place.

Will the ruby and the culprit be found? Or does only death await?

Gameplay

Explore the Isle of Ore by travelling to its many locations.

Converse with the residents, learning about them and their enemies.

Look around for clues and steal (er- borrow) evidence.

Solve puzzles and play mini-games to further the investigation.

Setting

Welcome to the Isle of Ore, home to butchers who will prepare ANY kind of meat without question, ... With an island full of criminals, anyone could be a suspect.

Characters

Have conversations with an eccentric cast of characters, each associated with one of the three gangs which rule over the isle. Be careful while interrogating or you might find yourself at their mercy.
